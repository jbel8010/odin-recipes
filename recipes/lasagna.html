Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that consists of layers of pasta, tomato sauce, meat, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Crushed tomato (or Tomato sauce)
  • Onion, chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Dried basil
  • Dried oregano
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Parmessan or mozzarella cheese
  • Egg
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef or Italian sausage until browned. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic and cook until the onion is translucent, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, and salt and pepper to the skillet.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg and ricotta cheese.
  6. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of the tomato sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Place three lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, slightly overlapping. Spread a layer of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, then sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Repeat this layering process, ending with a layer of tomato sauce and shredded cheese on top
  7. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
