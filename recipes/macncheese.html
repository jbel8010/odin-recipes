Mac and Cheese

Description

Mac and cheese, also known as macaroni and cheese, is a popular dish made from cooked macaroni pasta and a creamy cheese sauce. The dish originated in Italy but has become a popular comfort food in many countries, particularly in the United States.

Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni or other pasta
  • Butter or margarine
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese (such as cheddar, American, or a blend)
  • Salt and pepper (for seasoning)

Instructions

  1. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  3. Add the flour to the melted butter and whisk until smooth. Cook for 1-2 minutes until the mixture is lightly browned.
  4. Gradually whisk in the milk, making sure there are no lumps. Cook for 5-7 minutes or until the mixture thickens.
  5. Add the shredded cheese to the sauce and stir until melted and smooth.
  6. Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce and stir to combine.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy your homemade mac and cheese!/li>
